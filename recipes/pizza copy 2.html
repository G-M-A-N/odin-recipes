Pizza Recipes


Description

Pizza is one of the world's most well-known and popular dishes.
Pizzas are a symbol of many ancient societies around the Mediterranean Sea and cannot
be categorized as just another sort of meal.
Pizzas are available in a variety of forms and sizes.
Pizzas are typically circular and constructed of flat baked bread topped with a variety of toppings.

Pizza is known in the United States as pizza pie. Pizza can be described as an oven-baked,
flat, and generally round pizza. The pizza dough is often referred to as a pizza base or a crust
in the United States. Tomato sauce and cheese are the most typical toppings on a pizza foundation.

Ingredients

  • 2 cup all purpose flour
  • 100 ml tomato ketchup
  • 1 tomato
  • 2 onion
  • 1 teaspoon chilli flakes
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • water as required
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon dry yeast
  • 1/2 capsicum (green pepper)
  • 100 gm processed cheese
  • 4 mushroom
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella
  • 1 teaspoon oregano

Steps

  1. Preparation of the pizza dough
  2. Preparation of the pizza base
  3. Chopping of all the vegetables for the pizza
  4. Spreading the sauce and veggies on the base
  5. Baking the pizza at 250 degrees celcius for 10 minutes
