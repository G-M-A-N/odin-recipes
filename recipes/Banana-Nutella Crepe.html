crepe bar nutella


Description

If you haven't experienced Nutella, the incredible chocolate-hazelnut spread, go out and get a jar right now. Nutella is similar to how you can add cheese on almost anything to make it taste better. Read on for some fun ways to satisfy your chocolate cravings that aren't as boring as eating it straight from the jar. Melt some Nutella and pour it over ice cream for a quick hot fudge sundae if you're seeking something decadent. Nutella can also be used as an icing for cakes and cookies, providing the perfect balance of ease and simplicity.

Ingredients

  • 2 cup all purpose flour
  • 100 ml tomato ketchup
  • 1 tomato
  • 2 onion
  • 1 teaspoon chilli flakes
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • water as required
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon dry yeast
  • 1/2 capsicum (green pepper)
  • 100 gm processed cheese
  • 4 mushroom
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella
  • 1 teaspoon oregano

Steps

  1. Preparation of the pizza dough
  2. Preparation of the pizza base
  3. Chopping of all the vegetables for the pizza
  4. Spreading the sauce and veggies on the base
  5. Baking the pizza at 250 degrees celcius for 10 minutes
